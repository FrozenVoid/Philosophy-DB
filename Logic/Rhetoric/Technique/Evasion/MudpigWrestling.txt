Mud pig wrestling is a common rhetirical
technique that lowers discussion to lowest
common problems: mainstream politics/religion/economy,
which attracts low-quality posters("mud pigs")
eagerly supporting one or other side with
quantity of arguments that pressure more intelligent
posters to leave the area, instead of engaging
with low-level issues("mud pig wrestling") which
dominate with more controversial topics devolve
into heated debates where emotional arguments
 cannot be discussed peacefully:
 essentially turning debate into competition of
 emotional wills.
