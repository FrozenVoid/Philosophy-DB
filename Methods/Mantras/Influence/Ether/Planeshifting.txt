the power of mantras can shift their chanter subtle bodies
towards the plane which is linked with the mantra:
1.deity: the name of deity links to its plane/realm of control.
2.plane: the name of plane/heaven/hell/realm has portal-like quality
to link with its origin(also used in summoning).
3.elemental terminal: the last letter of a word e.g. L=ground in Hell,
shifts the word-chanter towards its elemental base(L=ground/Earth).
4.emotion/state link: the word describing an emotion/state/action has the power
to link the realm aligned with it.
5.transformation mantra: word which describe an animal/entity gain
spiritual link with it.
6.Concept word: the word describing a concept links to mental plane/subplane
which is connected with it.
