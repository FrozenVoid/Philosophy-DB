Prayer mudra(Anjali mudra) is
 a very common gesture that 
is associated with religion
(as it does aid spiritual growth).
Method:
Bring two palm close together 
with finger closed and straight,
pointing up like a prayer.

Effect:
One of most powerful mudras.
 Balance of all elements,
 spiritual growth/cleansing,
 Brings serenity and inner strenght.
Aids with communication with 
non-material entities/forces.
