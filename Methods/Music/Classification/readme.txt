Following methods will classify the music without any musical training or proficiency:
1. Proportion of time occupied by vocal element:
Vocal-only, vocal-heavy, vocal-centric, hybrid, instrument-centric, intrument-heavy, instrumental.
2. The pace of rhythm : speed of melody & song progression.
The pace is considered the average speed of the song/composition.
3. Complexity or polyphonality: number of simultaneous sound sources.
4. Melodicity: how much of the song is structured and how much is ambient/chaotic.
5. Beat centrality: how much of the melody depends on regular beats(is song centered on beat).
6. Emotional scale: is the song primary emotions negative, neutral or positive?
