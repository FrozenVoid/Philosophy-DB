Hidden crystal(stone) can have 
influence from its place as
area modifier.
Method:
1. Select a crystal for elemental
quality desired.
2. Optional: Add sigils(color
 must be same as chakra color
  influenced or stone color).
3. Charge its primary element 
with mantra or spells.
4. Hide/Bury the crystal in 
desired area.
5. Wait.
