Day(Yang primary pitta-kapha)
 Earth - 1/3 of sunrise to midday
 Water(kapha) - 2/3 of sunrise to midday
 Water(pitta) - 3/3 of sunrise to midday
 Fire - 1/3 of midday to sunset
 Air  - 2/3 of midday to sunset
 Ether -3/3 of midday to sunset
Night(Yin primary vata-kapha),
 Earth - 1/3 of sunset to midnight
 Water(kapha) - 2/3 of sunset to midnight
 Water(pitta) - 3/3 of sunset to midnight
 Fire - 1/3 of midnight to sunrise
 Air  - 2/3 of midnight to sunrise
 Ether -3/3 of midnight to sunrise
