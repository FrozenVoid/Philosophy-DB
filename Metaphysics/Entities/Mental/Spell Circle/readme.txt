A circle filled with sigils and geometric shapes, centered
on the caster or item(in summoning) that appears from astral
 energy shaped into a sigilized thoughtform(mental plane vortex?).
The circle can be protective(binding energy to place) 
or connecting(portal-like) towards some realm/entity.
Can be spinning or glowing(color hints at chakra involved).
Its borders can be further (materially) protected with salt, strong spices
or various items(e.g. colorful beads, small mirrors,ground glass, magnets).
