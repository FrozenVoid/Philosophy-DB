A "negative life trolley"/"euthanasia trolley"
 problem class
inverts the usual assumption that life itself
is a positive utility value, instead creating
scenarios where maximizing death increases
quality of life: e.g. 5 cancer patients in terminal
stage tied to tracks.

see https://independentaustralia.net/life/life-display/assisted-dying-solving-the-trolley-problem,10766
