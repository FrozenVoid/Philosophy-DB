Cheat sheet for morality systems:
Names for systems are not universally 
agreed upon, so follow the examples.

Morality System: Brief example of system
Utility - "The ends justify the means"
Intent  - "Good intentions justify the ends"
Black & White - "Was the action, intent & outcome good?"
Blue & Orange - "Was it funny?"
Ego-based - "Was it done for yourself?"
Flow  - "Was it planned?"
Novelty - "Was it original?"
Beauty - "Was it beautiful?"
Entropy - "Was it efficient use of energy to achieve the same goal?"
Least Burden - "Was the action necessary?"
Heroic - "Was this the best act in this situation?"
Progressive - "Was this action most beneficial for social progress?"
