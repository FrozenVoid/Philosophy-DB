Entities that lack concepts
of survival & resource aquisition,
will have morality systems
that differ considerably from
 conventional human morality(Black/White);
 The common umbrella terms is
  Blue and Orange morality:
  
e.g. interesting is good/boring is bad,
with amount of degrees of Good/Bad
not limited to 2, e.g.
morality can have components of
 sincerity+novelty+charm or 
 other non-moral characteristics that
 entities would judge as moral qualities.
