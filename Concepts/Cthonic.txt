An elemental quality of underground energy, associated with underworld/hell.
Opposite energy is Aetheric : refined air above, associated with heavens/mountains(Olympus).
A object/entity has Cthonic quality if it originates or draws energy from underground, with higher depths indicating higher Cthonic alignment(Underworld affinity).
A similar concept is Abyssal, originating from underwater depths.
