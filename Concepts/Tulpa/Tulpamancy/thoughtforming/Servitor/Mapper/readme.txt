Mapper is a servitor holding
 knowledge of locations/pathing/
 navigation in specific area,
 with multiple separate servitors
 adapted for specific areas.
Most such servitor develop 
through training and memorization.
 
 Types:
1.Navigator: learned patterns 
of navigations in specific area/
domain. Controls host during
 navigation process.
Also works in videogames,
location specific sports/games.

2.Pathing advisor: supplies
 associated information for
 current location and its features.
 Does not control host.

3.Integrator: supplies 
behavior patterns to adapt to
specific location, role-servitor
that associates location with role
and takes over host behavior.
